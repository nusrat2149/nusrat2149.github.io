Bengli New Year, better known as 'Pahela Baishakh'

Bengali New Year will be celebrared on Friday, April 15th, 2022

Bengalis celebrate the new year by greeting eachother with the saying "Shubho Noboborsho" which translates to "Happy New Year!" We wear new clothes, pray for a successful year, and eat traditonal and festive confectionery desserts. Many people parade the streets with banners, traditional clothes, listen to singers, and dance with celebration.

Eid al-Fitr

Muslims all around the world will be celebrating Eid al-Fitr on May 2nd, 2022 after a month long of Ramadan fasting.

This holiday depends on the cresent moon sighting. If the cresent moon is seen the night before, we have 29 days of Ramadan and the next day is Eid. However, if the cresent moon is not sighted, then we have one extra day of fasting and the following day is Eid. On Eid, Muslims cook and share their best food, dress their best in new clothes, pray the special Eid prayer in the morning, give charity, wear henna, and visit eachother's homes to celebrate.
